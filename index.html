Assistant Daily Checklist

Please complete all fields before submitting.

Studio Location
Opening — Studio area (before 10:00, hourly)

Clear all dust bins and garbage bins in all locations. Ensure all garbage and cartons from deliveries are thrown following and respecting building management disposal process

Clean the floor with mop and water/disinfecting solution (ensure to remove stains, spots or any dust in all corners of the studio)

Tree leaves to be cleaned daily

Air fragrance device is on always (settings PL17; refill)

Are all speakers fully charged and ready to use?

Office / Storage area

All supplies and stationery to be organized on the shelves. Personal belongings to be organized in assigned corner

First Aid Kit available (panadol, plaster, bandage, alcohol, sanitizer)

Water to be stored in assigned corner only. Vacuum the carpet

Reception

Dust cleaning (reception table, computer, shelves with placed printing products, table, sofa to arrange nicely)

Light on to create a cozy atmosphere

Chairs/sofas to be brushed, vacuumed and arranged

Garbage bin to be cleaned and placed new bag. Clean the YOU BABY STUDIO logo from dust — make sure it looks tidy

Mobile phone, batteries for camera to be placed on charge

On the table to be placed ONLY: work phones, laptop, credit card machine and stationery items

4 water bottles placed on coffee table

Refill tissue boxes — to be stored in proper tissue wooden box

Make sure the diffuser/fragrance is refilled and working — if no stock, ask to order

All printed products to be displayed and cleaned

Vacuum and clean the floor at the reception area

Toilet (hourly)

Make sure Ritual diffuser is refilled — if no stock, ask to order

Refill hand soap

5 hand towels to be displayed on the tray (small sizes ONLY)

Tissue paper to be replaced in tissue roller box

Toilet paper to be placed

Garbage bin to be cleaned and placed new bag

Toilet seats to be cleaned under the seat and top of the cover

Dust to be cleaned on top of tissue roller box

Mirror to be wiped

None of the cleaning tools to be placed in visible place

Kitchen area

Kitchen cupboard to be cleaned. Make sure the water dispenser is on and there is water in the bottle

Cross check cups, plates — cleaned and no stains left over

No dirty dishes in the sink (all dishes after cleaning to be wiped with dry towel and placed in the cabinet immediately)

Microwave after each use to be switched off

After each food warming to refresh the kitchen with air freshener

During shift

Check laundry bag and wash dirty clothes (12:00–15:00)

Iron clothes from previous day

Refresh suitcases: wash dirty backgrounds and outfits, steam remaining clothes and backgrounds, steam toys, pillows, beans

Packing suitcases for next day home services. Cross check schedule and prepare balloon setup (15:00–20:00)

Finalize schedule and share with Customer Coordinator (16:00–17:00)

Closing — 17:00 till 21:00

Unpack suitcases and props from the day and arrange them to assigned place

Cross check all props and verify if nothing is damaged. If yes, create incident report

Cake Smash & Newborn props and outfits must be washed immediately and removed from washing machine to dry for the next day

Reassure all switches are off, chargers removed from sockets across all the studio (do not leave anything switched on or on charge overnight)

AC, PC, lights are turned OFF before leaving

Clean studio before leaving: garbage bin to be cleaned and replaced with new bag, vacuum the floor

All props are placed back to their places
